Q: Who are The Bright Sparks?

Answer: The Bright Sparks are a group of professional musicians based in and around London, who have been rocking out at and playing amazing gigs at functions, events and weddings for nearly a decade. Every Bright Sparks gig is full of energy, packed with great, exciting tunes from five decades of pop and rock music, and is played with a style and charm that makes the band stand out from other run-of-the-mill function bands. Forget any preconceptions you may have about jaded, bland, head nodding musos, or naff wedding bands. That’s not what you’ll find here. The Bright Sparks are sharp, cool, switched on, and can really play.

Q: How long does the band play for?

A. Check-in time is at 3pm and check-out is at noon. We cannot guarantee either early or late check-in, but as with all special requests we will always note it in your reservation. If we have the availability, and a guest is not scheduled to check-in to your room, we can offer late check-out. Please note there is a half-day rate (half of your current rate) for staying in your room until 6pm, and checking out after 6pm warrants a full day rate (based on the rate you are paying). Again, late check-out is only based on availability and with confirmation from the front office.

02.Do we need to feed you?
